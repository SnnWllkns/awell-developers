## Compare use cases and effort

### Hosted pathway

This integration is ideal to quickly set up patient onboarding ([see example](https://orchestration-stories.vercel.app/stories/hosted-pathway)) or collect forms such as intake forms, PROMs/PREMs and pre-consultation questionnaires.

One engineer can set this integration up in less than a day. This makes it also ideal to test an end-to-end use case with Awell with minimal effort before considering other integration alternatives.

If your care flows/pathways are using scheduled steps to determine when activities are being activated or multiple stakeholders interacting in the same pathway, we recommend considering the Hosted activity custom integration.

[Learn how to set up a Hosted Pathway integration.](/awell-orchestration/docs/activities/hosted-pathway)

### Hosted activity

Typical use cases supported by this integration are longitudinal outcomes (such as PROMs / PREMs) collection, triage & eligibility flows and clinicial decision support.

This integration is more powerful, allowing the use of webhooks and taking full advantage of what Awell Studio has to offer. It is a good way to get the most out of the Awell platform with limited engineering effort.

UI customization is still limited with this integration. This is often enough for care team facing flows such as clinical decision support and guidelines. If you are looking for full control over your UI, for example for patient facing applications, please consider the custom integration.

[Learn how to set up a hosted activity integration.](/awell-orchestration/docs/activities/hosted-activity)

### Full integration

This integration offers the full power of Awell orchestration while ensuring full flexibility to define the patient and care team experience. Use this when you develop and manage your own care team and patient apps and want to integrate activities from complex care flows such as care flows/pathways seamlessly for your end users.

[View our examples stories and code examples](/awell-orchestration/docs/activities/custom-integration) to get started quickly with a custom integration.
