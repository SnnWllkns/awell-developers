---
title: What are Awell Extensions
description: Awell Extensions allow you to extend the functionality of the Awell Platform
---

Extensions allow you to extend the functionality of the Awell Platform. By providing a toolkit of SDKs and components, Awell allows developers to assemble their own piece of software to extend the capabilities of the Awell Platform.

## Extension categories

### Custom Actions

Build, test, and deploy Action Extensions to add custom actions to your care flows.
